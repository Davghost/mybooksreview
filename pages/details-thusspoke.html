Thus Spoke Zarathustra-Friedrich-Nietzsche

My review

Thus Spoke Zarathustra was unlike anything I had read before. At times it felt like poetry, at others like a deep conversation with a philosopher inside my own head. I didn’t understand everything, but that didn’t stop it from moving me. It challenged my beliefs and made me question what it means to grow, to lead, and to be free.
