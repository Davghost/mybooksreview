The Fall - Albert Camus

My review

The Fall by Albert Camus made me feel strangely exposed. It's like the narrator is not just confessing to someone in the book — he's talking directly to me. His words are sharp, ironic, and uncomfortable, but honest. It made me reflect on guilt, pride, and how we often wear masks without realizing it. It’s a short read, but it left a long echo in my thoughts.
