My review

Psycho-Cybernetics made me see how much our self-image shapes the way we live. It felt less like reading a self-help book and more like having a serious talk with someone who believes in your potential. Some parts felt outdated, but the core message hit me deeply: you become what you believe about yourself. It was simple, but powerful.
